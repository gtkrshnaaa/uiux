The Art of Slowing Down.

01. Temperature

Water at 92-94°C is ideal for extracting the delicate floral notes of our light roasts without bitterness.

02. The Bloom

Allow the coffee grounds to breathe for 30 seconds after the first pour. This releases gases and prepares the bean for extraction.

03. Intentionality

The speed of your pour dictates the body and clarity of the cup. Slow, concentric circles are key.
