The Alchemy of Light Roasting

At Bean & Bloom, we believe that coffee is a fruit. And like any fruit, it possesses delicate flavors that can be easily overwhelmed by too much heat. This is why we specialize in light roasting.

The roasting process is a dance with thermodynamics. We aim for the 'first crack'—the moment when the cell structure of the bean changes and its inherent sugars begin to caramelize. By stopping the roast shortly after this point, we preserve the bright acidity and floral aromatics that define high-altitude coffee.

"We don't roast to add flavor; we roast to reveal it."

It requires constant attention. Every 30 seconds, we monitor the temperature, the smell, and the color development. It is a slow, intentional process that reflects our overall philosophy of stillness.
